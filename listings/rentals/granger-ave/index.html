About This Home

1207 Granger Ave is the first-floor unit of a classic two-family home in Lakewood, offering 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, and 1,056 square feet of living space. A covered front porch welcomes you into a spacious living room centered around a wood-burning fireplace, flanked by a sunny corner sunroom with walls of windows.

The formal dining room features built-in shelving and flows into an eat-in kitchen equipped with a gas range, microwave, refrigerator, and plenty of cabinet storage. Both bedrooms offer generous closet space, and hardwood floors run throughout the unit.

A full-size washer and dryer are included in the shared basement laundry room. Outside, enjoy a fenced backyard and patio — all within walking distance of Lakewood's Detroit Ave shops, dining, and Lake Erie.
