About Hunter Foley

Real Estate Professional

I'm Hunter Foley, a licensed real estate agent with Berkshire Hathaway HomeServices Professional Realty, serving Cleveland and the surrounding areas since 2023.

My unique background combines healthcare expertise (licensed RN) with real estate knowledge. Whether you're a first-time homebuyer, looking to sell, or exploring investment properties, I bring professionalism, market knowledge, and genuine care to every transaction.
