About This Home

Welcome to 8662 Tanglewood Trail in the sought-after lake and beach community of Tanglewood, with award-winning Kenston Schools and Geauga County taxes. This beautifully updated 3-bedroom, 2.5-bath split-level home offers stunning views of the Tanglewood golf course fairway and lake.

Refinished hardwood floors flow throughout the main living areas, complemented by new luxury vinyl plank in the mudroom and living room. The open eat-in kitchen features granite counters and abundant natural light, while a cozy wood-burning fireplace anchors the family room. The first-floor office could easily serve as a fourth bedroom.

Upstairs, the owner's suite includes crown molding and a private bath, and the second-floor hall bathroom was fully remodeled in 2024 with a new tub, shower, vanity, tile work, and fixtures. The finished lower-level recreation room adds even more living space, plus additional storage.

Step outside to the brick patio and landscaped yard with a stunning water feature overlooking the golf course. This is Tanglewood living at its finest.
